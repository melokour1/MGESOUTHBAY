Warranty Coverage

Your peace of mind is important to us. That's why we back our work with warranties you can trust.

🛡

Parts & Labor

3 Years

All parts and labor are covered by our 3-year, unlimited mileage warranty. If something we repaired fails within that period, we'll fix it at no additional cost to you.

⌖

Wheel Alignments

1 Year

All wheel alignments are backed by a 1-year warranty. If your alignment goes out of spec within a year, bring it back and we'll realign it free of charge.

💰

Unlimited Mileage

No Limits

Unlike many shops that cap warranty at a certain mileage, our warranty has no mileage restrictions. Drive as much as you need with total confidence.

Our Warranty Promise

At MGE South Bay, we use only high-quality OEM and premium aftermarket parts. Combined with the expertise of our certified technicians, we are confident enough in our work to offer one of the best warranties in the industry.

Our warranty covers defects in parts and workmanship for the services we perform. Simply bring your vehicle back to our shop and we'll diagnose and resolve the issue at no charge if it's covered under warranty.

We believe a strong warranty reflects the quality of the work. When you choose MGE South Bay, you're choosing a shop that stands behind every bolt we tighten and every part we install.
