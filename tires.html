Hunter Precision Alignment

Our shop is equipped with a state-of-the-art Hunter alignment system — the industry's most trusted alignment technology. Proper alignment ensures your tires wear evenly, your vehicle handles correctly, and you get the best fuel economy.

Signs you may need an alignment include your vehicle pulling to one side, uneven tire wear, or a steering wheel that's off-center. We recommend getting an alignment check at least once a year or whenever you install new tires.

All alignments are backed by our 1-year warranty.

Nitrogen Tire Inflation
