Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Home / Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment through our website, we collect: your name, email address, phone number, and vehicle information (year, make, model). We may also collect any additional comments you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your appointment requests
  • Send you appointment confirmation and reminder emails
  • Contact you about your appointment or vehicle service
  • Improve our services

3. Information Storage

Your appointment data is securely stored using third-party services (Airtable and Google Sheets) that maintain their own security protocols. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Email Communications

When you submit an appointment request, you will receive a confirmation email and may receive a reminder email before your appointment. These are transactional emails related to your appointment, not marketing messages.

5. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at (310) 546-8999 or email SameDayAutoServices@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain appointment records for business purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
