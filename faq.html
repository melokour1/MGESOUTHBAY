Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

Home / FAQ
What are your hours of operation? +

We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM, and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you work on all makes and models? +

Yes! We service all makes and models, including domestic, import, hybrid, and light trucks/vans. Our technicians are trained and equipped to handle virtually any vehicle.

Can I bring my own parts? +

We do not install customer-supplied parts. This policy ensures that we can stand behind every repair with our full warranty. We source high-quality OEM and premium aftermarket parts at competitive prices, and all parts we install are covered under our 3-year warranty.

What is your warranty policy? +

We offer a 3-year, unlimited mileage warranty on all parts and labor. Wheel alignments are covered under a 1-year warranty. Visit our Warranty page for full details.

Do you offer courtesy rides? +

Yes! We offer complimentary local courtesy rides for all customers. If you need to get to work, home, or anywhere else locally while your vehicle is being serviced, we'll give you a ride.

How do I schedule an appointment? +

You can schedule an appointment by filling out our online form or by calling us directly at (310) 123-4567. Please note that the date and time you request may not be available — we will contact you to confirm your actual appointment details.

Do you provide estimates before starting work? +

Absolutely. We always provide a detailed estimate before beginning any work on your vehicle. We will explain what needs to be done and why, and we will not proceed until you approve the repair.

Are your technicians certified? +

Yes, our technicians are ASE certified and the majority are factory trained by manufacturers. They undergo continuous training to stay current with the latest automotive technology and repair techniques.

Do you service hybrid vehicles? +

Yes, we are hybrid certified. Our technicians have the specialized training and equipment needed to service hybrid vehicles safely and effectively.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and personal checks. We also offer financing options for larger repairs.

Still Have Questions?

Give us a call or stop by — we're always happy to help.
